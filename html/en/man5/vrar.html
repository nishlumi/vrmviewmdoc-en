18. VR and AR

From ver 2.3.0, it is compatible with VR devices such as Meta Quest and PICO. You can enjoy seeing VRM and various objects up close in VR/AR space, and create poses and motions in that space.

Note

As of ver 2.3.0, Meta Quest 3 and PICO4 are confirmed. Please note that both models are determined by the browser to be Android. Unity’s WebGL build is not officially supported on mobile, so we will be using it on VR devices as a trial implementation.

Please note that there may be cases where it does not work.
