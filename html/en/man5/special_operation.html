14.1. Operation with the keyboard

The keyboard operation in this application is summarized.

14.1.1. Main camera operation

Key

Operation

W

zoom in (forward)

S

zoom out (backward)

A

move left

D

move right

Shift + W

Rotate Up

Shift + S

Rotate down

Shift + A

Rotate left

Shift + D

Rotate right

F

Mmove up

V

Mmove down

Q

undo the rotation by the Z axis of the camera

M

Main camera (C)←→Current object (O) switching operation

R

Reset camera and center point

I

Separate the distance between the camera and the center point

O

Close the distance between the camera and the center point

Ctrl

Move the mouse while holding down Ctrl and left mouse click to rotate

SPACE

move the mouse while holding it down with the left click of the mouse

Note

You can change the speed of the camera during keyboard operation (WASD only) in the setting Movement speed of the camera during key operation.

14.1.2. Manipulating the current object

Key

Operation

W

Forward

S

Backward

A

move left

D

move right

F

Mmove up

V

Mmove down

Shift + W

rotate forward

Shift + S

rotate backward

Shift + A

turn left

Shift + D

turn right

Shift + F

rotate left

Shift + V

rotate right

Q

Return the rotation of IK to 0

G

Global (G)/local (L) switching ( for movement/rotation by key operation )

M

Main camera (C)←→Current object (O) switching operation

14.1.3. Manipulating IK markers on objects

Key

Operation

Ctrl

Ctrl + click IK markers to select multiple

X

Switch global and local coordinates (for IK markers)

T

Restore IK markers to their initial state (both movement and rotation)

Shift + Z

undo movement/rotation of IK

Shift + Y

redo movement/rotation of IK

14.2. Gamepad Controls

Added from version 2.10.0.

Key

Operation

left stick

Move forward, backward, left and right

Right stick

Rotate up, down, left and right

D-pad (up)

Move Up

D-pad (Down)

Move Down

L1

Switch the operation target to the main camera

R1

Switch the operation target to the currently selected object

Select

Each time you press it, the operating space switches between World and Local.

Note

The movement speed and rotation speed will use the Key operation setting.
