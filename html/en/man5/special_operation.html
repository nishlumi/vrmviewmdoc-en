13.1. Operation with the keyboard

The keyboard operation in this application is summarized.

Main camera operation

Key

Operation

W

zoom in (forward)

S

zoom out (backward)

A

move left

D

move right

Shift + W

Rotate Up

Shift + S

Rotate down

Shift + A

Rotate left

Shift + D

Rotate right

F

Mmove up

V

Mmove down

Q

undo the rotation by the Z axis of the camera

M

Switch Camera and Current object

R

Reset camera and center point

I

Separate the distance between the camera and the center point

O

Close the distance between the camera and the center point

Ctrl

Move the mouse while holding down Ctrl and left mouse click to rotate

SPACE

move the mouse while holding it down with the left click of the mouse

Note

You can change the speed of the camera during keyboard operation (WASD only) in the setting Movement speed of the camera during key operation.

Manipulating Current Objects

Key

Operation

W

Forward

S

Backward

A

move left

D

move right

F

Mmove up

V

Mmove down

Shift + W

rotate forward

Shift + S

rotate backward

Shift + A

turn left

Shift + D

turn right

Shift + F

rotate left

Shift + V

rotate right

Q

Return the rotation of IK to 0

G

global/local switching (For movement/rotation by key operation)

M

Switch Camera and Current object

Working with object IK markers

Key

Operation

Ctrl

Ctrl + click IK markers to select multiple

X

Switch global and local coordinates (for IK markers)

T

Restore IK markers to their initial state (both movement and rotation)

Shift + Z

undo movement/rotation of IK

Shift + Y

redo movement/rotation of IK
