6.13.2. Opening and closing the ribbon barÔÉÅ

You can collapse the ribbon bar by turning off the toggle switch on the right end of the ribbon bar. Supported from ver 2.6.0.

Closing the ribbon bar will make the WebGL screen wider.

opened state

closed state

Open tabs with ribbon bar collapsed

When the ribbon bar is collapsed and opened, the ribbon bar tabs will be displayed over the top of the WebGL screen and property panel.


In this state, you can close it by executing a menu item or clicking the panel name on the ribbon bar.
